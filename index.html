💡 Interview Tips

  • Research the company thoroughly before the interview - know their products, culture, and recent news
  • Practice answering out loud, not just in your head - record yourself or practice with a friend
  • Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral questions
  • Prepare 3-5 thoughtful questions to ask the interviewer about role, team, or company
  • Send a personalized thank you email within 24 hours after the interview
  • Review your resume and be ready to explain any gaps or transitions
  • Prepare a 60-second "Tell me about yourself" elevator pitch
